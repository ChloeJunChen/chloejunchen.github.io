About Me

Hi! I’m Chloe—a UX researcher and 5th-year PhD candidate at Purdue University. With an MS in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) specializing in UX Design, a minor in Psychology, and rigorous PhD training, I combine a user-centered mindset with strong research and problem-solving skills. I’m passionate about understanding user needs, designing meaningful experiences, and shaping lives to be easier, happier, and healthier.

My research spans walkable urban design, sound design for tour guide apps, website design to evoke luxury or approachability, and generative AI for personalized communication. I use both qualitative and quantitative methods, including user interviews, experiments, statistical modeling, and topic modeling for big data. Through collaborations, publications, and internships, I’ve been fortunate to contribute to impactful UX projects.
