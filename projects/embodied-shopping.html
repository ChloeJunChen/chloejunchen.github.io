Embodied Shopping Experience

Redefining in-store grocery shopping with innovative embodied design solutions.

Background

Modern grocery stores often struggle with customer satisfaction due to issues like overcrowded areas and difficulty in locating staff for assistance. These challenges disrupt the shopping experience, making it stressful and time-consuming for customers. This project aimed to design an embodied solution to address these pain points.

Research Questions

  • How can we help customers navigate overcrowded areas more effectively?
  • How can we improve the process of requesting and receiving assistance in-store?

Phase 1: Exploring Initial Ideas

Research Methods

  • Literature Review: Investigated the psychological impacts of lighting colors to identify red as a signal for alert and blue as a calming indicator for assistance.
  • Interviews: Conducted 12 interviews with grocery shoppers to identify pain points such as difficulty avoiding crowds and finding help.

Key Insights

  • Shoppers needed a real-time solution to visualize crowded areas in the store.
  • Calming signals were preferred to avoid stress while waiting for help.

Phase 2: Iteration and Testing

Research Methods

  • A/B Testing: Tested high-intensity vs. low-intensity lighting to determine the most comfortable and effective visibility.
  • Usability Testing: Evaluated the functionality of a mobile app prototype for navigating crowded areas and signaling assistance needs.

Key Insights

  • Low-intensity lights were more comfortable for users while maintaining visibility.
  • Users strongly preferred the mobile app over static kiosks for navigation.

Phase 3: Final Design and Results

Final Design Decisions

  • Red lights on the ceiling to highlight overcrowded areas in real time.
  • A mobile app providing a store map with live updates on crowded sections.
  • Blue lights to signal customer assistance requests.

Results

  • 92% of users found the red/blue light system intuitive and effective.
  • 85% of users reported the mobile app significantly improved their navigation experience.
  • Employees responded 30% faster to customer assistance requests with the blue light system.
